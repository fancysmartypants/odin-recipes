Cacio e Pepe

Description:

This is the recipe for Cacio e Pepe.

It is a great dish for those who love cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Dried Shitake mushroom
  • Corn meal, or polenta
  • Parmesan

Steps:

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to boil
  2. Add 1 1/2 cup of corn meal
  3. When the corn meal thickens, lowers the heat and cook for 40 min.
  4. Soak dried shitake mushroom for 30 mins, after which, sear it until crispy
